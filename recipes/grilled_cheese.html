Grilled Cheese


A Grilled Cheese recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slice cheddar cheese
Steps
  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side-down onto skillet bottom and add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side-up on top of sandwich. Grill until lightly browned and flip over; continue grilling until cheese is melted.
